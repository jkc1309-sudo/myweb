Deployment & Impact

Systems developed for public-facing museum and heritage contexts

AI guide and museum narrative systems were deployed or designed for Tengwang Pavilion, Jinggangshan Revolutionary Museum, Haihunhou Archaeological Site Park, August 1st Nanchang Uprising Memorial Museum, Liaoning Provincial Museum, Western Xia Imperial Tombs, Nanyue King Museum, and Liu Shaoqi Memorial Site.

A related mini-program reached 154,000 users, demonstrating that the work has moved beyond conceptual prototyping into real public engagement.
